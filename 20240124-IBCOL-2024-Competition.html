International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) Season 2024 Launches on UN International Day of Education

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE24 January 2024

In a fitting tribute to the transformative power of education, the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) announces the commencement of its 2024 season on this year’s International Day of Education. This global event underscores the critical role of educational initiatives in advancing technology and innovation worldwide.

The 2024 season of the IBCOL invites students and young professionals from around the globe to engage in the challenge of harnessing blockchain technology for creative and practical solutions to real-world problems. The event aims to foster a deeper understanding of blockchain technology and its potential applications, while also promoting a spirit of international cooperation and knowledge exchange.

Season Highlights:

  • Participants will compete by developing original blockchain solutions across various sectors, including finance, healthcare, and sustainability.
  • The season will culminate in the prestigious IBCOL 2024 finals, to be held in Amsterdam, where finalists will present their projects to a panel of international experts.

Empowering the Next Generation:
Launching the season on the International Day of Education highlights IBCOL’s commitment to educational excellence and its role in empowering the next generation of technology leaders. IBCOL not only challenges participants to think critically and innovatively but also provides a platform for exceptional educational and networking opportunities.

Call to Action:
Educational institutions, technology enthusiasts, and students worldwide are encouraged to participate in this enriching experience. Registration details and competition guidelines are available on the IBCOL official website.

For further information about IBCOL 2024 and how to participate, please visit www.ibcol.org.

About the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL):
The International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is a global competition that promotes the study and practical application of "blockchain", which includes but is not limited to cryptography, distributed ledger technology, decentralized applications, and web3 solutions. Through its annual events, IBCOL brings together young talents to solve pressing challenges and contribute to technological advancement in the blockchain space.
