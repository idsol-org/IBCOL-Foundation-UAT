IBCOL Working Groups

To develop codes of conduct, best practices, and standards for blockchain, cryptography, and web3 areas, especially in emerging technologies, through collaborative dialogue, research, and publications by groups of individuals and entities from academia, industry, and government ("working groups")

Web3 Cryptography Working Group

This IBCOL Working Group explores cryptography and cryptographic engineering for applications & infrastructures in web3.

Web3 Governance Working Group

This IBCOL Working Group explores governance principles, frameworks, and tech for building legal & compliant web3 applications & infrastructures.

Web3 Trust & Privacy Working Group

This IBCOL Working Group explores research and development in cybersecurity and privacy enhancing technologies for application in web3.

IDSOL Foundation (International Data Science Olympiad)

IDSOL Foundation is an EU non-profit elevating intelligence and competence in data science, including machine learning and artificial intelligence, and promoting practical solutions to foster a global community of practitioners and to empower young people through education so that they make informed career choices.
