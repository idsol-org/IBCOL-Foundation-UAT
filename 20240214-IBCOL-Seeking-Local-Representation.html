International Blockchain Olympiad Seeks Country Partners for IBCOL 2024 in Amsterdam

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE14 February 2024

The International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL), a leading global competition dedicated to innovation and education in "blockchain" (inclusive of cryptography, distributed ledger technology, decentralized applications, and web3 solutions), is actively seeking country partners to represent and participate in the upcoming IBCOL 2024. The 7th finals of the International Blockchain Olympiad are scheduled to take place in the vibrant city of Amsterdam, bringing together the brightest minds from around the world.

IBCOL invites educational institutions, technology associations, and industry leaders to become country partners, tasked with forming and nominating a delegation to represent their nation at this prestigious event. This partnership offers a unique opportunity to showcase local talent on an international stage and contribute to the global blockchain community.

Opportunity for Partners:
Country partners will play a crucial role by:

  • Identifying and assembling a team of top students and young professionals with a passion for blockchain technology.
  • Providing these teams with the resources and support needed to develop their innovative projects.
  • Nominating the teams to compete in the IBCOL 2024 finals.

Benefits of Partnership:
Country partners will benefit through:

  • Enhancing your organization’s visibility and prestige in the blockchain and technology sectors.
  • Fostering innovation and talent development within your country.
  • Building and strengthening international networks with other leaders in the blockchain community.

Event Details:
More information shall be released in the coming months:

  • Event: 7th International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL 2024)
  • Location: Amsterdam, The Netherlands, EU
  • Date: Week of 11 November 2024

Interested parties are encouraged to express their interest and request more information about becoming a country partner by contacting the IBCOL organizing committee here

About the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL):
The International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is a global competition that promotes the study and practical application of "blockchain", which includes but is not limited to cryptography, distributed ledger technology, decentralized applications, and web3 solutions. Through its annual events, IBCOL brings together young talents to solve pressing challenges and contribute to technological advancement in the blockchain space.
